Linear Regression

Walt Disney and Box Office Success

The "Walt Disney Success" project analyzes 579 Disney movies to identify success factors. Top-grossing films show growing popularity of action and adventure genres. A regression model confirms their positive impact on box office performance and the confidence intervals support the correlation. Let's expect more action, adventure, Marvel, Star Wars, and live-action films in the future!

Time Series

Tracking Stardom: Kardashian-Jenner Popularity Analysis

This time-series project utilizes Google Trends data to analyze the popularity of the Kardashian-Jenner sisters. It employs visualizations to showcase trends over time, emphasizing significant events like Kim Kardashian's rise and Kylie Jenner's emergence. The analysis also compares the popularity of the Kardashian and Jenner family lines, revealing a balanced trend since 2015.
